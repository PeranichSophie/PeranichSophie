Beer App UX

Google UX Wireframe: Crafting Beer Tracking App Screens

In the Google UX Wireframe project, our focus was on creating wireframes for a beer tracking app. The purpose was to design wireframes for a couple of screens, setting the foundation for an intuitive and user-friendly beer tracking experience.

Key Highlights:

  • Beer Tracking App: The project revolved around conceptualizing and visualizing the user interface for a beer tracking application.
  • Wireframe Creation: The outcome was a set of wireframes that laid out the structural framework for the app, ensuring a clear and efficient user experience.
These wireframes serve as the blueprint for a beer tracking app that promises to be both user-centric and functional, simplifying the process of tracking and managing your favorite brews.
