Vertige: Transforming Vertigo Tracking

In the project for Vertige, a vertigo tracking app, we set out to overcome several challenges and enhance the user experience. The goal was to make it more user-friendly, visually pleasing, and informative.

Key Improvements:
  • Enhanced Usability: I streamlined navigation, making Vertige more user-friendly for hassle-free vertigo tracking.
  • Visual Appeal: A fresh design palette has breathed new life into the app, enhancing the overall user experience.
  • Detailed Tracking: Users can now record detailed vertigo episodes, capturing symptoms, triggers, duration, and more for better insights.
  • Valuable Resources: We introduced dedicated pages for VeDA and trigger foods, providing users with informative resources.
  • Efficient Scheduling: With the addition of a calendar and daily summaries, users can easily monitor their vertigo patterns.
The standout feature is the detailed tracking and graphing capabilities, which offer users a visual representation of their vertigo journey, making it easier to identify patterns and triggers. In conclusion, Vertige has undergone a remarkable transformation, making it more user-centric and informative. The addition of detailed tracking and insightful graphs empowers users to manage their vertigo more effectively, ultimately improving their quality of life.
